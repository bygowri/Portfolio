About me

As a young creative mind, I realized my interest to enhance and reimagine things. During my architecture studies, I discovered the influential power of design on individuals and communities, sparking my interest in Product Design. Excited by its challenges and opportunities, I began developing my skills after graduation, grateful for the solid foundation provided by my architectural background.

My diverse creative outlets, including digital art, reading, watching thought-provoking movies, and traveling, along with my passion for wildlife and nature observation, inspire and ground me both personally and professionally.
